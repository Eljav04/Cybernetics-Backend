Visa Information

When planning your travels, understanding visa requirements is a critical step to ensure smooth and hassle-free journeys. A visa is an official document issued by a country that grants you permission to enter, stay, or leave their territory for a specific period. It is typically attached to your passport and is essential for maintaining security and legal protocols for international travel.

Why Do Travelers Need a Visa?

A visa ensures that you meet the entry requirements of the destination country, preventing unauthorized entry.

It allows governments to monitor who is entering their country, ensuring the safety of their citizens and visito

Different visas are issued based on the purpose of travel—tourism, business, study, or work.
